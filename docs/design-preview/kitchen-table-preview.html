Lamb Shoulder with Pomegranate & Mint

B
by Bart · Updated 3 days ago
30m
Prep
3h
Cook
6
Serves
5.0
Rating
Slow-roasted until falling apart, finished with a bright pomegranate molasses glaze and fresh herbs from the garden. A centerpiece dish that fills the house with incredible aromas.
Ingredients
  • 1.5 kglamb shoulder, bone-in
  • 3 tbsppomegranate molasses
  • 2 tbspolive oil
  • 1 headgarlic, halved
  • 2 tspcumin, ground
  • 1 tspcinnamon
  • handfulfresh mint leaves
  • 100 gpomegranate seeds
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 160C. Score the lamb shoulder in a crosshatch pattern and rub generously with olive oil, cumin, cinnamon, salt and pepper.
  2. Place the lamb in a deep roasting tray with the halved garlic head. Cover tightly with foil and roast for 3 hours until the meat is falling off the bone.
  3. Remove foil for the last 30 minutes. Brush with pomegranate molasses and increase heat to 200C to caramelize the glaze.
  4. Rest for 15 minutes, then scatter with pomegranate seeds and torn fresh mint. Pull apart with two forks and serve family-style.
