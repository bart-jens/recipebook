Lamb Shoulder with Pomegranate & Mint

by Bart
Published 2026-01-14
3:30
Time
6
Serves
Med
Level
5.0
Rating
Ingredients
  • 2 kg Lamb shoulder, bone-in
  • 80 ml Pomegranate molasses
  • 1 bunch Fresh mint leaves
  • 50 g Pine nuts, toasted
  • 4 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Ground cumin
  • to taste Salt and black pepper
Method
01

Score the lamb shoulder deeply, rub with olive oil, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper. Let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.

02

Brush generously with pomegranate molasses. Place in a roasting tin with 200ml water, cover tightly with foil.

03

Roast at 160C for 3 hours, basting every 45 minutes. Remove foil for the final 30 minutes to develop a sticky glaze.

04

Rest the lamb for 15 minutes. Pull into rough chunks, scatter with torn mint leaves and toasted pine nuts. Drizzle with pan juices.
