## Description

EefEats is a recipe platform where you can save, organize, and share your favorite recipes.

Import recipes from any website, Instagram post, or cookbook photo. Create and edit your own recipes from scratch. Rate recipes as you cook them and build a personal log of everything you make.

Follow friends and see what they're cooking. Browse public recipes from other home cooks, fork and personalize them, and share your own creations with the community. Organize everything with custom tags and find recipes with full-text search.

Features:

- Import recipes from URLs, Instagram posts, and cookbook photos
- Create and edit recipes with structured ingredients and step-by-step instructions
- Rate recipes (1-5 stars) and log every time you cook
- Organize with custom tags, favorites, and collections
- Unit conversion (metric and imperial)
- Follow friends and see their cooking activity in your feed
- Discover public recipes from other cooks in the community
- Fork recipes and personalize them with your own tweaks
- Private or public profile options with follow request approval
- Full-text search and filtering by tags, course type, prep time, and more
